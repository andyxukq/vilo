Frequently Asked Questions

Can I clean Vilo with alcohol wipes?

We don’t recommend it. Use mild soap and water, then dry thoroughly.

Can I wear Vilo in the shower or sauna?

Check the latest guidance on our support page. “Everyday exposure” is fine; high heat and prolonged water exposure may not be.

My ring looks dull—what should I do?

Use the polishing care pad gently, and avoid abrasive surfaces going forward.
