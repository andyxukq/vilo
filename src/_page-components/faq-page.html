Frequently Asked Questions

The Essentials

What is Vilo?

Vilo is a next-skin ring and companion app that helps you understand your rhythms—sleep, recovery, daily energy, and cycle-aware context—without turning your life into a dashboard.

Is Vilo a medical device?

No. Vilo is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

That said, we take science seriously: our sensing stack and many of our algorithms are built against established research methods and validated standards where applicable. We aim for clinical-level rigor in the engine, while keeping the experience human and calm.

Do I need a subscription?

Yes—advanced insights require ongoing compute and AI processing.

Vilo hardware and core features work out of the box, and Membership unlocks deeper software features and advanced insights.

As a thank-you to early supporters, we include free Membership for a limited time (see “Membership” and “Pre-Order Offers” below). Quiet revolution, together.

Tech Specs

What sensors does Vilo use?

Vilo uses a multi-sensor stack designed for continuous, low-power sensing:

Red + infrared LEDs monitor blood oxygen trends

Green + infrared LEDs track heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)

A digital temperature sensor captures nightly temperature shifts

An ultra-low-power accelerometer tracks movement and daily activity, day and night

Does Vilo measure blood oxygen?

Vilo monitors blood oxygen trends for wellness context. It is not intended for medical diagnosis.

Why does Vilo track temperature shifts at night?

Nightly temperature trends add context to sleep, recovery, and rhythm—especially when you look at patterns over time, not a single night.

Does Vilo track workouts?

Vilo tracks movement and activity. Workout experiences may vary depending on app features and integrations.

“Sensors, chips… even the glue?”

Yes. We use top-tier components and precision manufacturing throughout.
Also: please don’t take it apart. If you do anyway, at least take a moment to admire the engineering.

Size, Weight & Fit

How wide and thick is Vilo?

Width: approx. 5.5 mm
Thickness: approx. 2.5 mm

How much does it weigh?

Approx. 5.1–7.1 g depending on ring size—roughly the feel of a light everyday ring (not a bulky gadget).

How do I choose my size?

We strongly recommend using the Vilo Sizer Set. Wear a sizer for at least 24 hours, including sleep, and choose the size that feels secure without discomfort.

I’m between sizes—what should I do?

Want a more secure fit for sleep and movement? Size down.
Prefer easier removal or your fingers swell often? Size up.
If you’re unsure, message us—fit is personal.

Wearing & Comfort

Can I wear Vilo all day and night?

Yes. Vilo is designed for continuous wear, especially during sleep. If you prefer breaks, that’s fine—just know trends work best with consistency.

Is it water resistant?

Vilo is water resistant for everyday exposure like hand washing and light rain. For showering, swimming, or sauna use, follow our Care guidelines.

Will it scratch?

All wearables pick up signs of life. Vilo is made with durable materials and coatings, but scratches can happen with hard contact (keys, gym equipment, stone, metal edges). Treat it like a favorite accessory: wear it, enjoy it—don’t sandpaper it.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

Vilo is designed for skin contact, and we test materials and formulations carefully—including interior/exterior finishes and bonding materials. If you have known allergies or very sensitive skin, check our Materials section and stop wearing it if irritation occurs. When in doubt, consult a clinician.

App & Insights

What does Vilo measure?

Vilo tracks signals such as heart rate trends, HRV, temperature trends, blood oxygen trends, and motion—and, with AI, turns them into insights about sleep, recovery, and daily energy.

Why do my numbers change day to day?

Because you’re not a spreadsheet. Sleep, stress, travel, meals, caffeine, exercise, and cycle shifts can all change signals. Vilo focuses on trends so you can see what’s stable vs what’s just a day.

Can I control how much Vilo talks to me?

Yes. You can adjust notification frequency, insight depth, and what categories you want to see. Quiet is the default; control is yours. Over time, our goal is that you can trust the assistant more—and manage less.

Does Vilo work without my phone nearby?

Yes, within limits. Vilo can store data and sync when your phone reconnects. Some real-time features may require proximity.

Can I export or delete my data?

Yes. We respect your privacy and your control. You can request export or deletion in the app or by contacting support. We’ll verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

Battery & Charging

How long does the battery last?

Up to 14 days depending on usage and settings. Even with frequent daily use, most users will see 7+ days. Battery capacity may naturally decline over time—like any rechargeable device.

How long does charging take?

Fast charging typically reaches full charge in 20–80 minutes, depending on starting battery level.

Will charging breaks ruin my insights?

No. Vilo is built around rhythm and long-term trends. Short charging breaks won’t break your story.
