The Essentials

What is Vilo?

Vilo is a next-skin ring and companion AI agent that helps you understand your rhythms—sleep, recovery, daily energy, and cycle-aware context—without turning your life into a dashboard.

What’s included with Vilo (Co-Founder Pre-Order Edition)?
  • Vilo Ring
  • Vilo “Dark Planet” portable charger
  • USB-C charging cable
  • Vilo Sizer Set (designer-made sizing rings)
  • Polishing care pad
Is Vilo a medical device?

No. Vilo is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.
That said, we take science seriously: our sensing stack and many of our algorithms are built on established research methods and validated standards where applicable. We aim for clinical-level rigor in the engine, while keeping the experience human and calm.

Do I need a subscription?

Yes—advanced insights require ongoing compute and AI processing.
Vilo hardware and core features work out of the box, and Membership unlocks deeper software features and advanced insights.

Trial, Returns & Exchanges

How do returns work?

Submit a return request within the trial window, then send the product back following our instructions. Please include all items from your order (ring, charger, cable, care pad, and sizers). Returns are processed after inspection. Full details are on our Support page.

Do you offer exchanges?

We currently don’t offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, please return your original order (within the return window) and place a new order for the correct size.

How do I contact support?

Email support@vilo.co or contact us via the Support page. If your request is order-related, please include your order number.
