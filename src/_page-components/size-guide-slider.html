Step 1 — Order the Kit
Place your order and select “Free Sizing Kit.”
We ship the kit first, so your Vilo arrives fitting the way it should.
Step 2 — Receive the Kit
Delivery typically takes 3–8 days, depending on your location.
Inside is a full set of sample sizers — and yes, it looks like a box of beautiful rings. We made it fun on purpose.
Step 3 — Wear, Live, Sleep
Pick the finger you plan to wear Vilo on. For best accuracy, the index finger is ideal—middle and ring fingers also work well. Wear your sizer for 24 hours, including sleep. If it disappears on you—in the best way—you’ve got your size.
Step 4 — Tell Us Your Size
Once you decide, go to My Orders and submit your size. No account yet? Create one using the email you used at checkout.
And yes—feel free to pass the kit to a friend. Good taste travels.
For more help finding your perfect ring size, please refer to our Sizing Guides.
