Simple returns. Clear rules.

If Vilo isn’t right for you, you can return it within your trial window.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return:

  • Your return request must be submitted within 30 days (standard) or 60 days (Founder) of delivery
  • You must return all included items (ring, charger, cable, sizers, care pad, etc.)

    • Light signs of wear from normal trial use are fine. Significant damage may affect eligibility.

How to return

  • Submit a return request via our Support page
  • We’ll send return instructions (and a label if applicable)
  • Pack everything securely and ship it back
  • After inspection, we’ll process your refund

Refund timing

Refunds are processed after inspection and may take [X business days] to appear, depending on your bank.

Not eligible

  • Purchases made outside our website
  • Gift cards
  • Final sale items (if any)
