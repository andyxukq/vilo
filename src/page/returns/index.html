Return with peace of mind.

Simple returns. Clear rules.

If Vilo isn’t right for you, you can return it within your trial window—no long emails, no awkward explanations. We designed the trial for real life: sleep, work, travel, stress, calm, routine, chaos.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a return:

Your return request must be submitted within 30 days (standard) or 60 days (Founder terms) of delivery

You must return all included items (ring, charger, cable, sizers, care pad, etc.)

Light signs of wear from normal trial use are fine. Significant damage may affect eligibility.

How to return

Start your return

Submit a return request through our Support page. Include your order number and a short note on what you’re returning (and why, if you’d like—optional).

Get instructions

We’ll email you the next steps, including the return address and shipping guidance. If a prepaid label applies in your region, we’ll include it.

Pack it all

Place the ring and all included items back in the box (charger, cable, sizers, care pad, etc.). Pack securely so everything arrives safely and the ring isn’t scratched in transit.

Refund after check

Once it arrives, we’ll inspect the return and process your refund. You’ll get a confirmation email when it’s approved and issued (bank timing varies).

Refund timing

Refunds are issued after inspection (usually 1–3 business days after we receive your return).

Once issued, your bank may take 5–10 business days to post the credit.

We’ll email you when your return is received and when the refund is processed.

Not eligible

Purchases made outside our website (please follow the seller’s policy)

Gift cards (non-refundable)

Final sale items (marked at purchase)
