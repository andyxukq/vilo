Treat it like a favorite accessory.

Vilo is made to be worn daily. A little care keeps it looking (and working) its best.

Cleaning

A little routine care keeps Vilo looking refined—and keeps charging reliable.

Soft cloth, often

Wipe regularly to remove oils and dust.

Mild soap + water, when needed

For deeper cleaning, use gentle soap and rinse lightly.

Dry before charging

Pat dry and let it air-dry completely before placing it on the charger.

Water & Heat

Vilo is designed for everyday exposure (hand washing, light rain). For showering, swimming, or sauna use, follow the latest guidance in the app/support page.

Scratches & Daily Wear

All wearables pick up signs of life. Avoid storing Vilo in a pocket with keys or coins, and be mindful around stone, metal edges, and gym equipment.

Charging Care

A little attention here helps protect both the finish and the battery over time.

Clean, dry contacts

Keep the charger and ring contacts free of moisture, lotion, or dust.

Never charge wet

If Vilo is damp, dry it fully first—no exceptions.

Skip the heat

Avoid charging in hot cars, direct sun, or near heaters while charging.

Skin Comfort

If irritation occurs, remove Vilo and let skin recover. Clean the inner surface and ensure a proper fit. If symptoms persist, consult a clinician.
