Treat it like a favorite accessory.

Vilo is made to be worn daily. A little care keeps it looking (and working) its best.

Cleaning

  • Wipe with a soft cloth regularly
  • Use mild soap and water when needed
  • Dry thoroughly before charging

Water & heat

Vilo is designed for everyday exposure (hand washing, light rain).

For showering, swimming, or sauna use, follow the latest guidance in the app/support page.

Scratches & daily wear

All wearables pick up signs of life.

Avoid storing Vilo in a pocket with keys or coins, and be mindful around stone, metal edges, and gym equipment.
