AI & Personalization

A gentle guide that learns you.

Vilo’s intelligence isn’t here to judge your body. It’s here to help you understand it—clearly, calmly, and in a voice that feels like yours.

AI as a bridge inward

AI is just another tool—one that can help us go inward, or far out. With Vilo, it’s a quiet bridge: it turns scattered signals into something you can actually use, without turning your day into a performance.

How Vilo creates guidance

Vilo combines three things:

  • Your signals over time (trends, not snapshots)
  • A curated wellness knowledge base (built from established research and common practice)
  • Your preferences — what you care about, how you like to read, what you want to ignore

That’s how it becomes personal. Not just “smart,” but specifically helpful to you.

Choose your reading style

Some days you want one sentence. Some days you want context. Vilo adapts—short summaries when you’re busy, a little more when you’re curious, deeper only when you ask. You can also tune the tone: direct, warm, minimalist, analytical—supportive, never pushy.
