Materials & Craft

Made to be chosen.

Vilo isn’t designed to look like a gadget. It’s designed to look like taste—cool, refined, and a little unexpected.

A quieter lineage of taste.

Most modern tech products speak the same language—cold titanium, glossy plastic, and a one-note engineer’s aesthetic. Vilo takes a different path—borrowing from craft traditions where objects were made to be personal, kept, and worn with intention.

Beauty, woven across centuries.

Our design draws from a long human memory—the gentle glow of Chinese jade, the quiet restraint of ancient Egyptian adornment—and carries that centuries-old sense of beauty into the present, reformed through modern material craft.

A soft glow, built in layers.

To achieve Vilo’s signature translucent, softly diffused, micro-matte finish, we use a layered approach: a PC base for structure, a medium-to-high diffusion masterbatch for that gentle glow, in-mold texturing for depth and fewer fingerprints, and a micro-matte hard coat for durability with a refined sheen—never shiny, never loud.
